🕵🏻 Guess the Book Title! 📕

ℹ️ Rules
  • '( )' Emojis inside parentheses form a conceptual or symbolic word when combined (e.g. (🤏🐎) -> Little + Horse = Pony
  • '[ ]' Emojis inside brackets form a literal compound word (e.g. [🌞🌻] -> Sun + Flower = Sunflower
  • Parentheses and brackets can be combined
  • 's' means plural (e.g., 🐶s = dogs)
  • Emojis represent only the title and not the plot/author/characters
  • You may not know all the books, a little help from Google is allowed 😉
